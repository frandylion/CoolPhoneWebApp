user
- user_id <serial> (pk)
- user_last_name <varchar>
- user_first_name <varchar>
- password <varchar>

user_plan
- user_id <int> {fk: user}
- plan_type <varchar> {fk: plan}
- payment_type <varchar> {fk: plan}

call_log
- call_id <serial> (pk)
- user_id <int> {fk: user}
- duration <decimal>
- call_date_time <datetime>
- call_type <varchar> {fk: call_type} [local, long-distance]

data_log
- user_id <int> (pk) {fk: user}
- start_date <date> (pk)
- end_date <date>
- data_used_mib <int>

call_type
- type_name <varchar> (pk) [local, long-distance]
- cost_per_minute <decimal>

plan
- plan_type <varchar> (pk) [basic, premium]
- payment_type <varchar> (pk) [pre-paid, post-paid]
- call_limit_min <int>
- data_limit_mib <int>
- data_cost <decimal> [total monthly data cost]

bill
- bill_id <int> (serial)
- user_id <int> {fk: user}
- due_date <date>
- balance_due <decimal>
- plan_type <varchar> {fk: plan}
- payment_type <varchar> {fk: plan}

bank
- user_id <int> (pk) {fk: user}
- bank_account_num <int> [pdf says we need 'basic bank info']
- card_num <int>
- balance <int>

transaction
- transaction_id <serial> (pk)
- bill_id <int> {fk: bill}
- paid_date <date>
- new_balance <decimal>
